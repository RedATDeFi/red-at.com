SECURITY HEALTH CHECK

THE GOAL OF A CYBERSECURITY DIAGNOSTIC IS TO HELP YOU IDENTIFY YOUR WEAKNESSES IN CYBERSECURITY AND PROTECTION OF SENSITIVE DATA. WE WILL DELIVER A SIMPLE AND ADEQUATE ACTION PLAN THAT WILL ALLOW YOU TO REINFORCE YOUR ORGANIZATION'S CYBER DEFENSE LEVEL TO MINIMIZE THE IMPACT OF CYBER ATTACKS.

PENETRATION TESTING & VULNERABILITY ANALYSIS

INTRUSION TESTS AND VULNERABILITY ANALYSIS DIFFER IN THEIR OBJECTIVE. INDEED, THE OBJECTIVE OF A VULNERABILITY TEST IS TO DETECT KNOWN VULNERABILITIES ON SYSTEMS, WHILE THE OBJECTIVE OF AN INTRUSION TEST IS TO SIMULATE AN ATTACK, BY DISCOVERING AND EXPLOITING VULNERABILITIES

SIMULATION ATTACKS

THE SIMULATION OF ATTACKS IS A FORMIDABLE MEANS OF MEASUREMENT, IT MAKES IT POSSIBLE TO DISCOVER THE TRUE CYBERDEFENSIVE LEVEL OF ITS EMPLOYEES, TO THUS HAVE A REALISTIC OUTLINE OF THE REFLEXES AND DECISIONS EACH ONE FACING THE TARGETED CYBERATTACKS.
